About Us

Gridtech-Sistem (GTS)

Egyptian-Spanish Company, Specialized in Signaling and Telecom Installation Services for Railway & Metro. Covering all new Traffic and Mobility Control and Management Solutions, ITS Facilities, Smart Mobility Solutions.

Located in Spain, Egypt and UAE, with more than 1000 Experts, handling more than 1500 KM Railways Installations in the past 5 years globally.

In Egypt, we are leading the Railway Signaling Market, being top partner for Thales, Siemens, and Alstom. With highest Egyptian market share handling Assuit Nagaa Hamadi Project in Upper Egypt, Cairo Alex and Cairo Benha Projects in Delta, and Benha Portsaid Project.

In UAE, we handled metro Dubai expo 2020.

In Spain we have the highest market share, with different installations for High Speed Trains in Madrid, and other ERTMS Installations in Spain.
