Airports

We are the lead company in CNS/ATM system integration, airports infrastructures and Mission Critical Communications Systems. We are also offer services and supplies such as the Flight Field, Beaconing and Operational Safety.

With a highly specialized technical team, we guarantee our clients the best results in any of the project phases: definition of requirements, design, execution, configuration, maintenance and operation.

  • Monitoring and Control Systems Software, based on SCADA platforms, for any equipment with possibility of exploitation and remote supervision.
  • Systems Monitoring Software, Control and Recording / Reproduction of Communications (ED-137B Certified)
  • Radio communications TETRA, DMR, MW Links.
  • FIDS (Flight Information Display System) and Multiservice Networks and Passenger Services.
  • Video Surveillance System, Perimeter Security, Access Control and Environmental Control (SIG.Security).
